1. Choose Quality Cocoa

Start with a good-quality chocolate or cocoa powder for a deep, authentic chocolate flavor. Dark chocolate or Dutch-processed cocoa powder works well.

2. Create a Chocolate Base

Mix cocoa powder or melted chocolate with a little hot water to make a chocolate “base” or syrup. This step ensures that the chocolate is fully dissolved before adding another ingredients.

3. Add Sweetener Gradually

Adjust sweetness to taste. Start with a small amount of sugar, honey, or syrup and increase as desired, depending on the bitterness of the chocolate.

4. Add Fresh Milk

To make the drink extra refreshing, use cold milk or a mix of milk and cream for creaminess. Dairy-free alternatives like almond, oat, or coconut milk also work well

5. Finish With a Topping

Top your iced chocolate with whipped cream, a sprinkle of cocoa powder, or chocolate shavings for added texture and richness based on your preferences.
